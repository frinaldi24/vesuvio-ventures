**Thesis (one line).** Most of how a company works is never documented; capture that as a "Context Graph" and you become the system of record beneath enterprise AI.

**Why we're in (small).** Founder with a relevant exit (Boxplot → Hyperscience); a huge prize if the memory layer becomes critical infrastructure; domain-savvy backers (Bek Ventures, an early UiPath investor).

**What has to be true.** The Context Graph is a real, compounding moat — not a feature the hyperscalers ship next year — and enterprises adopt it despite compliance friction.

**Biggest risk.** OpenAI/Microsoft/ServiceNow build agent-memory into the platform; no ground truth makes quality hard to audit; GDPR friction on ingesting bank/insurer data.

**The plan.** Small initial check; follow-on strictly gated on proof the moat compounds and on a real paid full launch. Kill if a hyperscaler ships a good-enough layer or paid retention is weak.
