**Thesis (one line).** Europe is rearming and demands sovereign capacity; a Palantir-alumni team building the defence backbone is early to a generational, European-only spending wave.

**Why we're in.** Exceptional mission-credentialed team (ex-Palantir); a durable macro tailwind (~€800B EU defence by 2030); a sovereignty mandate that structurally excludes US incumbents; a top-tier syndicate (Founders Fund, Lakestar, General Catalyst) for a pre-product seed.

**What has to be true.** The team converts the sovereignty argument into real contracts, focuses on a winnable beachhead, and survives a brutally capital-intensive build.

**Biggest risk.** Pre-product with a vast scope; slow, fragmented, incumbent-driven European procurement; far better-funded competitors (Anduril, Helsing).

**The plan.** Small, high-asymmetry position; **1x reserves** strictly gated on a first real contract/funded pilot. Resolve the investor-operator co-founder governance. Kill if no meaningful contract in 18–24 months.
