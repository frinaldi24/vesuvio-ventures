**Thesis (one line).** Whoever solves durable, uniform RNAi delivery into the brain from a simple injection owns an entire class of undruggable CNS diseases.

**Why we're in.** Elite, proven team (ex-Ochre Bio CEO; the scientist who started Alnylam's brain-delivery program); specialist syndicate (EQT Life Sciences, age1); a real, strategically-prized problem with pharma acquirers already paying billions for CNS delivery.

**What has to be true.** The brain shuttle delivers a therapeutic dose across the barrier, distributes deep and uniformly, and silences the target durably — none of which is yet proven.

**Biggest risk.** Delivery simply doesn't work at dose. This is a binary science bet.

**The plan.** Small strategic entry, **1.5x reserves** to double down at Series B *only if* the pre-clinical delivery readout justifies it. Kill and write down if brain exposure is sub-therapeutic with no chemistry fix.
