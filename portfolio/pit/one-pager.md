**Thesis (one line).** Enterprises don't want chatbots — they want software that quietly does the manual work; a repeat category-building team is early to that shift.

**Why we're in.** 3 of Voi's 4 cofounders + Klarna/iZettle alumni; pre-empted by a16z with a marquee angel list; real European industrial + sovereign-tech tailwind. Classic founder-led opportunistic bet.

**What has to be true.** "Governed deployment" is a durable moat, margins move from services-like to software-like, and traction extends well beyond the founders' network.

**Biggest risk.** A crowded, well-funded field, and a forward-deployed-engineer model that ends up a great consultancy rather than a venture-scale software company.

**The plan.** Priced seed entry; **1x reserves** for a Series A gated on arm's-length net revenue retention and improving gross margins. Kill if revenue stays founder-network-concentrated and margins stay services-like.
